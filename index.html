Your clients’ data, locked down tight

You’re trusting us with your clients’ most sensitive information. We built the platform from day one to make sure that trust is deserved — not as a checkbox, but as architecture.

PII Vault

Phone numbers, emails, and addresses are never stored alongside business data. They live in a separate encrypted vault (AES-256). The rest of the system only sees anonymous reference IDs — so even if something were breached, no personal data is exposed.

Your Data Is Yours Alone

Every organization’s data is walled off at the database level. It’s not a filter in the app that could have a bug — the database itself enforces it. Your data is physically invisible to every other account on the platform.

Encrypted End to End

Everything is encrypted — in transit and at rest. Passwords, tokens, credentials — nothing is ever stored in plain text. Your data is protected whether it’s moving between systems or sitting in storage.

No Personal Data in Logs

System logs and error reports never contain names, phone numbers, or email addresses. Only anonymous IDs. Even our own engineers can’t accidentally see your clients’ personal information while troubleshooting.

Minimal Access, Full Control

When you connect Gmail or other accounts, we only ask for the permissions we actually need. Tokens are encrypted and used only for what you authorized. Disconnect anytime — tokens are immediately revoked and deleted.

Built for Compliance

GDPR, CCPA, LGPD — the platform is designed to help you meet your obligations. Configurable data retention, full export and deletion, audit trails on every change. Data Processing Addendums available on request.

Built for regulated industries

You’re trusting us with your clients’ most sensitive information. We built the platform from day one to make sure that trust is deserved — not as a checkbox, but as architecture.

PII Vault

Phone numbers, emails, and addresses are never stored alongside business data. They live in a separate encrypted vault (AES-256). The rest of the system only sees anonymous reference IDs — so even if something were breached, no personal data is exposed.

Your Data Is Yours Alone

Every organisation’s data is walled off at the database level. Not a filter in the app that could have a bug — the database itself enforces it. Your data is physically invisible to every other account on the platform.

Minimal Access, Full Control

When you connect Gmail or other accounts, we only ask for the permissions we actually need. Tokens are encrypted and used only for what you authorised. Disconnect anytime — tokens are immediately revoked and deleted.

Privacy-Aware by Design

The architecture supports right-to-erasure (“forget me”) out of the box — one action removes a person’s data cleanly across the platform. Data retention is configurable, data can be exported or deleted, and changes are logged. Transcripts are PII-redacted before storage.
